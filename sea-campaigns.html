What is SEA?

SEA (Search Engine Advertising), also known as PPC (Pay-Per-Click) or Google Ads, is paid advertising on search engines. When someone searches for keywords related to your business, your ad appears at the top of Google — above all organic results.

Unlike SEO, which takes months to build momentum, SEA delivers instant visibility. Turn on a campaign today, and you can start getting clicks, calls, and customers within hours.

The catch? Running successful Google Ads campaigns requires expertise. Poorly managed campaigns waste money on irrelevant clicks. That's where we come in.

At Morfye, we design, launch, and optimize Google Ads campaigns that maximize your ROI. Whether you want more website traffic, phone calls, form submissions, or online sales — we create campaigns engineered to deliver results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I budget for Google Ads?

It depends on your industry, competition, and goals. We typically recommend starting with at least €500-1,000/month in ad spend, plus our management fee.

How quickly will I see results?

Unlike SEO, Google Ads deliver instant traffic. You can start getting clicks and leads within hours of launching. However, optimization takes 1-2 months to maximize ROI.

What's the difference between SEO and SEA?

SEO is organic (free clicks) but takes 3-6 months to see results. SEA is paid (you pay per click) but delivers instant traffic. Ideally, use both together for maximum visibility.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No. We offer month-to-month contracts. However, we recommend at least 3 months to properly optimize campaigns and see the best results.

What's your management fee?

Our management fee is typically 15-20% of your monthly ad spend, with a minimum of €299/month. This covers strategy, setup, optimization, reporting, and ongoing support.

Can I run Google Ads myself?

You can, but it's easy to waste money without expertise. Google makes it simple to spend money but hard to spend it wisely. Most businesses find better ROI by hiring professionals.

What if the campaign doesn't work?

We provide transparent reporting and work closely with you to optimize performance. If a campaign truly isn't delivering, we pivot strategy or recommend alternative channels.
