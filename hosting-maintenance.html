Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between hosting and maintenance?

Hosting is the server space where your website files live. Maintenance includes updates, backups, security monitoring, and support to keep your site running optimally.

Can I bring my own domain name?

Absolutely! You can keep your existing domain and point it to our hosting, or we can help you register a new one.

What happens if my website goes down?

Our 24/7 monitoring system alerts us immediately. We investigate and resolve the issue as quickly as possible — usually within minutes.

Do you offer email hosting too?

Yes! We can set up professional email addresses (like you@yourbusiness.com) connected to your domain.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. We offer flexible month-to-month plans with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.

How much does hosting and maintenance cost?

Plans start at €29/month for basic hosting and go up based on your site's complexity and traffic. Contact us for a custom quote.

What if I need changes to my website?

That's part of maintenance! Minor updates (text changes, new images) are included. Larger changes (new pages, features) can be quoted separately.
