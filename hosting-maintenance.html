Frequently Asked Questions

What types of web hosting do you offer?

We offer managed web hosting services tailored to small and medium businesses. Unlike generic hosting companies, our hosting plans are fully managed — meaning we handle server configuration, security, and maintenance for you. We use cloud hosting infrastructure with SSD storage, scalable bandwidth, and high uptime guarantees.

What is the difference between shared hosting, VPS, and cloud hosting?

Shared hosting is the cheapest option — your website shares server resources with many others, which can hurt performance and uptime. VPS hosting gives you a dedicated slice of a server with more control. Cloud hosting distributes your site across multiple servers for maximum uptime and scalability. We use cloud hosting because it combines performance with scalability — without the high cost of dedicated servers.

Do you support WordPress, Joomla, and other CMS platforms?

Yes — WordPress hosting is one of our specialties. We also fully support Joomla, Drupal, and any PHP-based CMS or ecommerce platform (WooCommerce, Magento, PrestaShop). We manage updates, plugin updates, theme updates, and security patches as part of our maintenance plans.

What uptime guarantee do you offer?

We target 99.9% uptime for all hosting plans. Our 24/7 monitoring system alerts us the moment your site goes down, and we act immediately. Uptime is critical — every minute of downtime costs you potential customers.

What's the difference between hosting and maintenance?

Web hosting is the server where your website files live — it's the foundation that keeps your site online. Maintenance covers everything else: plugin and CMS updates, security scans, daily backups, performance optimization, SSL renewal, and technical support. Our hosting packages include both.

Can I bring my own domain name?

Absolutely. You can keep your existing domain names and point them to our hosting. We can also register new domains for you, or transfer your existing domain. Some hosting packages include a free domain name. We handle all DNS configuration to get your site up-and-running quickly.

Do you offer email hosting too?

Yes — we can set up professional business email addresses (like you@yourbusiness.be) connected to your domain as part of our hosting services.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. We offer flexible month-to-month hosting plans with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. No lock-in, no hidden fees.

How much do your hosting plans cost?

Hosting plans start at €29/month for our standard managed hosting package, which includes SSL, daily backups, 24/7 monitoring, and basic maintenance. Higher-tier packages with more resources and priority support are available. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you offer webmail and FTP access?

Yes. All hosting plans include professional webmail so you can access your business email from any browser. FTP access is available for file transfers. We also provide SSH access for technical users — root access is managed by our team to keep infrastructure secure.
