What is SEO and Why Does It Matter?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your website to rank higher in search engine results — primarily Google. When someone searches for services you offer, SEO determines whether your website appears on page 1 or buried on page 10.

Here's the harsh truth: 75% of people never scroll past the first page of Google results. If your website isn't ranking well, you're invisible to potential customers actively searching for what you offer.

Good SEO doesn't just bring more traffic — it brings targeted, high-quality traffic from people ready to buy. It's one of the most cost-effective marketing strategies because you're not paying for every click like with ads.

At Morfye, we combine traditional SEO with modern GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) to ensure you're visible everywhere your customers are looking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. You'll typically see initial improvements in 3-6 months, with significant ranking gains after 6-12 months. It's not instant, but the results compound over time.

What's the difference between SEO and paid ads (Google Ads)?

Paid ads give instant visibility but stop the moment you stop paying. SEO takes longer but provides lasting, cost-effective results. Ideally, you use both together.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings on Google?

No one can ethically guarantee specific rankings—Google's algorithm is too complex. We focus on data-driven strategies that consistently improve rankings and drive measurable traffic growth.

Is local SEO different from regular SEO?

Yes. Local SEO focuses on ranking in specific geographic areas (like "Brussels" or "Brussels") and includes optimizing your Google Business Profile, local citations, and location-specific content.

How much does SEO cost?

It varies based on competition, goals, and scope. Basic local SEO starts around €299/month, while comprehensive national SEO campaigns can be €1,000+/month. We provide custom quotes.

Can I do SEO myself?

You can learn the basics, but professional SEO requires technical expertise, ongoing research, and time. Most business owners find it more effective to hire experts while they focus on their core business.

What happens if I stop SEO services?

Your rankings won't disappear overnight, but competitors will eventually overtake you. SEO requires ongoing maintenance to stay ahead as algorithms and competition evolve.
