What is SEO and Why Does It Matter?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of optimizing your website to rank higher in results across Google, Bing, and other search engines. When people search for services you offer, your SEO ranking determines whether you appear on page 1 or buried on page 10.

Here's the harsh truth: 75% of people never scroll past the first page of Google results. If your website isn't ranking well, you're invisible to potential customers actively searching for what you offer.

Good SEO doesn't just bring more traffic — it improves your conversion rate by attracting targeted visitors ready to buy. It's one of the most cost-effective marketing strategies available, because unlike paid ads you're not paying for every click. Traffic from organic SEO compounds over time.

At Morfye, we combine traditional SEO with modern GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) to ensure you're visible everywhere your customers are looking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. You'll typically see initial improvements in 3–6 months, with significant ranking gains after 6–12 months. Unlike PPC (pay-per-click), organic search results from SEO compound over time — you're not paying for every click.

What are the most important SEO tactics?

An effective SEO strategy uses: keyword research to find what your customers search for; on-page SEO (title tags, meta descriptions, URLs); technical SEO to ensure your pages are indexed correctly; link building to earn high-quality backlinks; content marketing to target long-tail keywords; and local SEO for geographic targeting.

What is the difference between on-page and off-page SEO?

On-page SEO covers what's on your web pages — title tags, headings, URLs, meta tags, keyword phrases, internal linking, and content quality. Off-page SEO is about your authority across the web — backlinks, inbound links, and external mentions. Both are essential for strong search engine rankings.

What SEO tools do you use?

We use Google Search Console, Google Analytics, SEMrush, and Ahrefs to track search queries, monitor backlinks, analyze competitors, and measure your SEO performance. We provide monthly reports so you always know where you rank.

What's the difference between SEO and paid ads (Google Ads)?

Paid search (Google Ads, PPC) gives instant visibility — you pay per click and appear at the top of results immediately. Organic search from SEO takes longer but delivers lasting, cost-effective results without paying per click. Ideally, use paid search for quick wins while SEO builds your long-term foundation.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings on Google?

No ethical SEO company can guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithm is too complex. We focus on data-driven techniques that consistently improve search rankings, grow organic traffic, and deliver measurable ROI. We track and report every improvement.

What is the difference between white-hat and black-hat SEO?

White-hat SEO follows search engine guidelines — quality content, legitimate link building, proper technical optimization. Black-hat SEO uses manipulative tactics like keyword stuffing and link schemes. While black-hat tactics can show short-term gains, they risk Google penalties. We exclusively use white-hat techniques.

Is local SEO different from regular SEO?

Yes. Local SEO focuses on ranking in specific geographic areas (like Brussels or Belgium) for location-based queries. It includes optimizing your Google Business Profile, building local citations, and targeting location-specific keywords. It's one of the most cost-effective strategies for small businesses.

How much does SEO cost?

It varies based on competition, your niche, and scope. Basic local SEO starts around €299/month, while a comprehensive national campaign can be €1,000+/month. We provide custom quotes after an SEO audit.

What happens if I stop SEO services?

Your rankings won't disappear overnight, but competitors who keep optimizing will eventually outrank you. SEO requires ongoing content marketing, link building, and technical maintenance to stay ahead as algorithms evolve.
