What is SEO and Why Does It Matter?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your website to rank higher in search engine results — primarily Google. When someone searches for services you offer, SEO determines whether your website appears on page 1 or buried on page 10.

Here's the harsh truth: 75% of people never scroll past the first page of Google results. If your website isn't ranking well, you're invisible to potential customers actively searching for what you offer.

Good SEO doesn't just bring more traffic — it brings targeted, high-quality traffic from people ready to buy. It's one of the most cost-effective marketing strategies because you're not paying for every click like with ads.

At Morfye, we combine traditional SEO with modern GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) to ensure you're visible everywhere your customers are looking.
