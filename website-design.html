Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new website cost?

It depends! A simple one-page website will be less than a large e-commerce store. We provide a custom, no-obligation quote after our initial discovery call.

How long will it take to build my website?

A typical business website takes 4-8 weeks from start to launch, depending on the complexity and how quickly we receive content and feedback from you.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Absolutely! We build on easy-to-use platforms (like WordPress or Webflow) and provide full training so you can manage content, add blog posts, and more.

Do you provide website hosting and maintenance?

We can. We offer optional monthly care plans that include secure hosting, daily backups, security updates, and technical support to keep your site fast and safe.

What is the difference between SEO and GEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps you rank high on Google search results. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) helps you get recommended by AI assistants. We build our sites to be experts at both.
