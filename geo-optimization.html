What is GEO?

Online visibility has changed dramatically — organic search traffic from SERPs has shifted as traditional clicks from search engine results have fallen by over 55% since 2022. Searchers now ask AI assistants instead of clicking through search engine rankings. Businesses relying only on Google search rankings are losing clients without even realizing it.

GEO is the evolution of optimizing for search in the AI era. While SEO focuses on ranking factors like title tags, meta descriptions, keywords, and backlinks to improve your Google ranking — GEO ensures AI chatbots like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google Gemini recommend YOUR business when people ask for help.

More people are now asking AI assistants instead of Google. If your content isn't optimized for AI indexing, you're invisible to this growing audience — even if your SEO ranking and organic traffic are strong.

Improve your competitive position now — GEO is not just the future, it's happening today. Businesses that adapt early see more conversions while competitors wonder where their clients went.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does GEO take to show results?

Initial improvements can be seen in 2–4 weeks. Full optimization typically takes 2–3 months as AI engines index and understand your updated content, structured data, and inbound authority signals.

How is GEO different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO helps your web pages rank in organic results on Google through on-page SEO (title tags, meta descriptions, headers), technical work (crawl optimization, mobile-friendly design), and off-page signals (backlinks). GEO targets AI assistants directly — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity — which don't use the same ranking factors as Google. A complete digital marketing strategy needs both.

Does GEO help with local search?

Yes — local GEO is one of the most powerful tactics for local businesses. When searchers ask AI 'best web designer near me' or 'accountant in Brussels,' you want to be the answer. We optimize your local presence so AI engines know your niche, location, and services.

What is the role of content marketing in GEO?

Content marketing is the backbone of GEO. AI engines are trained on text — the more high-quality, relevant content your web pages have, the better AI understands and recommends you. We focus on content that avoids duplicate text, targets long-tail queries, and uses proper meta descriptions and title tags.

Do you build backlinks for GEO?

Yes — citation building, backlinks, and inbound links from authoritative websites are central to our GEO process. AI engines learn from the web — when trusted sources mention your business, you become more credible to AI. This work overlaps with traditional SEO and improves both your search rankings and AI recommendations.

Will GEO replace my current SEO efforts?

No. GEO complements your SEO strategy — you still need traditional SEO for Google rankings, PPC for paid search, and content marketing for inbound traffic. GEO adds a new layer of visibility so that when AI assistants are asked about your niche, your business is recommended.

Which AI engines do you optimize for?

We optimize for ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), Perplexity, Google Gemini, and Microsoft Copilot. Each has different signals — some rely on web crawling, others on their training data — so our tactics are tailored per platform.

Is GEO worth it for local businesses?

Absolutely. Local GEO is incredibly powerful for small businesses. When someone in your niche asks an AI assistant for a recommendation, you want to be the answer for your city or region — whether that's Brussels, Ghent, Antwerp, or beyond.

How much does GEO cost?

We offer one-time GEO setup starting at €499, or monthly GEO management from €199/month. Custom packages available for larger businesses or competitive niches.

What industries benefit most from GEO?

All industries with search queries benefit, but especially: consultants, lawyers, accountants, healthcare providers, real estate agents, local services, and B2B businesses where potential clients use AI to search for and evaluate options before contacting a service provider.
