What is GEO?

Online visibility has changed dramatically — traditional click-through rates have dropped by more than 55% since 2022. Why? Because people now ask AI assistants instead of clicking search results. Businesses relying only on Google are sinking without even realizing it.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the evolution of traditional SEO for the AI era. While SEO helps you rank on Google, GEO ensures AI chatbots like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google Gemini recommend YOUR business when people ask for help.

Think about it: More people are now asking AI assistants "Who's the best tax consultant in Brussels?" or "Recommend a web designer in Belgium" instead of Googling. If your website isn't optimized for AI, you're invisible to this growing audience.

GEO is not just the future — it's happening NOW. Businesses that adapt early will dominate their markets while competitors wonder where their clients went.
